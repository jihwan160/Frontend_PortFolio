CAN I AMEND MY ORDER?

Once your order is placed, it is not possible to amend it. However, if you should need to send anything back once received, please Click here to view our full returns processes and policies.

HOW DO I REDEEM MY ONE HOTSPUR PROMO CODE?

If you have a One Hotspur Membership, you will have received a promo code in an email for either ₩‌22,500 or ₩‌30,000 depending on your Membership subscription. You can redeem this against nearly everything on the Spurs Shop Online by entering the code at the Basket Page before you enter the Checkout. Please note you cannot split the payment and use some of your voucher across more than 1 transaction. You will lose the balance otherwise.

HOW DO I GET MY MEMBERSHIP DISCOUNT?

When you purchase an Adult Membership pack from Tottenham Hotspur, you will receive a ₩‌22,500 unique voucher code to spend on the Online Spurs Shop. You can enter this code at the basket page to receive your discount.
