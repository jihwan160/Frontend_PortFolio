The 27th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the fourth film in Phase 4.
And it is the third work in the Marvel Studios Spider-Man series,
and the final chapter of the Homecoming trilogy.

Spider-Man 'Peter Parker', whose identity is exposed to the world due to 'Mysterio''s scheme, loses his normal daily life overnight. In order to solve the problem, he goes to 'Doctor Strange' to ask for help, but as the multiverse opens unexpectedly, uninvited guests from different dimensions appear. Peter Parker' is faced with the worst crisis ever in the powerful attack of 'Doctor Octopus' and other enemies who have a deep grudge against Spider-Man...
